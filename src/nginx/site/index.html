About the Breed

The Great Pyrenees, also known as the Pyrenean Mountain Dog, is a large working breed historically used to guard flocks in the Pyrenees Mountains. They are known for their thick, weather-resistant coats and steady, patient temperament.

Temperament

  • Calm, confident, and independent
  • Affectionate with family; reserved with strangers
  • Protective instincts as natural guardians

Care & Grooming

  • Regular brushing for their dense double coat
  • Daily exercise and mental stimulation
  • Early socialization and consistent training

History

Originating in the Pyrenees Mountains, these dogs partnered with shepherds to protect flocks from predators. Their calm vigilance and imposing presence made them legendary livestock guardians.
